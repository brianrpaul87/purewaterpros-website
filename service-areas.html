Communities served

Serving Greater Victoria, the West Shore and the Saanich Peninsula.

Pure Water Pros provides scheduled residential and light-commercial water-treatment service across Greater Victoria, including Victoria, Langford and the West Shore, the Saanich Peninsula, Sooke and nearby communities. Availability depends on the type of work, travel requirements and appointment capacity.
