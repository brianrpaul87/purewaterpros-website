Website information practices

Privacy policy.

This policy explains how Pure Water Pros handles information submitted through this website and ordinary technical information produced when the site is used.

Effective date: July 11, 2026

Information you provide

The pre-launch contact form may collect your name, email address, phone number, postal code, water source, requested service and the details you choose to provide about your water, home or existing equipment.

Technical information

The website host may automatically record standard server information such as the date and time of a request, browser type, device information, referring page, requested pages and internet protocol address. If analytics tools are added later, this policy will be updated as appropriate.

How information is used

  • To respond to questions and pre-launch service requests.
  • To prepare for an assessment, service visit or quotation.
  • To operate, secure and improve the website and contact process.
  • To maintain necessary business and service records.

Sharing

Pure Water Pros does not sell customer contact information. Information may be shared with service providers that support website hosting, email or business operations, or when disclosure is reasonably required by law. Information may also be shared with a qualified trade or laboratory when you authorize or request that coordination.

Retention and security

Information is kept only as long as reasonably needed for the purpose for which it was collected, normal business records, security, dispute resolution or legal requirements. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no website or email system can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may ask to review, correct or delete personal information held by Pure Water Pros, subject to legitimate recordkeeping and legal requirements. You may also choose not to provide optional information through the form.

External services

This website may link to external services such as email, maps, social platforms or review sites. Those services operate under their own privacy practices.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to info@purewaterpros.ca.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when website tools, business processes or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are made.
