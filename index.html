A better sales process

Test first. Explain clearly. Recommend only what fits.

The goal is not to sell the biggest equipment package. It is to understand the water, the home, the current system and the result you actually need.

  1. 1
    Describe the problem

    Start with the symptom, water source and any equipment already installed.

  2. 2
    Test and inspect

    Review relevant water readings, flow, plumbing and site conditions.

  3. 3

    Understand what each option solves, what it does not solve and the expected maintenance.

  4. 4
    Install and verify

    Confirm performance after installation and leave the system understandable to the homeowner.
